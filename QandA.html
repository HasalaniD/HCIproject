Does this keyboard have a physical volume control?

It doesn't have volume control out of the box but you can very easily add it. Google a program called 3RVX and download it from their site. Once downloaded type 3RVX in the windows search bar and RIGHT click then select open folder. Go into the settings program and on the top open the settings tab. Now you just click the + and select volume up then assign it to any key you want (one you don't use like next track) and repeat the process for volume down. Make sure you check "start with windows" under the general tab so the changes will work after you shut down your PC. It takes about 1 minute from start to finish and works great. You can add all sorts of functionality with 3RVX but I only use it for volume
